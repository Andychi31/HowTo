How to Throw a Curveball

What is a curveball?

The curveball is a very usual pitch in baseball that a pitcher can throw to get the batter to chase and strike him out. It is a pitch that breaks to right for left-handed pitchers, and breaks to the left for right-handed pitchers. It has been used for centuries of baseball, and still used by almost every pitcher today. Today, I am going to teach you how to throw the curveball. However, it is not easy.

How to Throw the Curveball

There is a specific way to grip the baseball, and a specific way to throw the curveball to make it curve. In this little how to, I will teach you both how to grip the ball, and what way to throw the ball. I will also provide pictures and other sources to show how to as well. There are rules to how to throw a curveball:

  • First of all, it takes years of practice.
  • You have to grip the ball a certain way to force the ball to spin how you want it to spin.
  • The more the spin, the better the curve.
